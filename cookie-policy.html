Cookie Policy

This website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience and to analyze website traffic. By continuing to use this site, you consent to the use of cookies in accordance with this policy.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser. They help websites remember your preferences and activity to improve your experience.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies solely for the purpose of collecting anonymous data through Google Analytics. This data helps us understand how visitors interact with our site—such as which pages are visited, how long users stay, and how they found the site.

Google Analytics

Google Analytics collects information such as your device type, browser, and general geographic location (not your precise address). This data is anonymized and cannot be used to identify you personally.

Learn more about how Google uses data from websites: Google’s Partner Sites Policy.

Managing Cookies

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. For more information, visit www.allaboutcookies.org.
